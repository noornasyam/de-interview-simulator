# Databricks V2 Question Bank

This folder contains the curated Databricks question bank for the DailyDEHub AI Interview Coach hybrid question system.

## Status

- Beginner: complete, 10 questions
- Junior Data Engineer: complete, 10 questions
- Mid-Level Data Engineer: complete, 10 questions
- Senior Data Engineer: complete, 10 questions

Total: 60 questions.

## Coverage

The bank focuses on Databricks data engineering interview concepts:

- Spark fundamentals and DataFrames
- Delta Lake, Delta tables, Delta log, and ACID transactions
- Medallion architecture with Bronze, Silver, and Gold layers
- Unity Catalog governance, catalogs, schemas, permissions, lineage, and audit
- Job clusters, interactive clusters, SQL warehouses, and cluster sizing
- Auto Loader and Structured Streaming
- Delta Live Tables and data quality expectations
- Performance tuning, file compaction, Z-ordering, and small-file management
- Security, governance, reliability, cost optimization, and enterprise platform operations
- Multi-workspace strategy and enterprise Databricks architecture

Beginner and Junior questions focus mostly on objective concepts. Mid-Level mixes objective and practical scenarios. Senior, Lead, and Architect levels emphasize production design, troubleshooting, governance, cost, security, reliability, and enterprise architecture.
