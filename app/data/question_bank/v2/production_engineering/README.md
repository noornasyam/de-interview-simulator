## Coverage

The bank focuses on production readiness and operations for data engineering platforms:

- Monitoring, alerting, logging, observability, metrics, and dashboards
- Incident management, RCA, runbooks, on-call, and escalation
- SRE fundamentals: SLI, SLO, error budgets, reliability, and availability
- Disaster recovery, backup/restore, rollback, and recovery testing
- Capacity planning, cost optimization, cloud operations, and operational excellence
- CI/CD, release management, rollback strategy, and change management
- Data pipeline operations, Composer operations, Vertex operations, and BigQuery operations
- Governance, ownership, security operations, and executive reliability reporting

Beginner and Junior questions focus on operational concepts and terminology. Mid-Level emphasizes scenarios, troubleshooting, and RCA. Senior, Lead, and Architect levels focus on reliability strategy, incident leadership, operational governance, DR, SRE, and platform operations.
